Profile

Hello,this is me.

My name is Jorge Zevallos with Peruvian- Spanish nationality. I was born in northern Peru in the city of Trujillo on 11/18/1982.

I started studying in 2000 the career of Systems Engineering because I like technology and specially programming was something that caught my attention.

I moved to Barcelona-Spain in 2007 and there is where I began to feel attracted by the design. I have taken courses in web design, but most of my knowledge was self-taught in my spare time where I dedicated myself to creations of web pages, social networks, designs logos and designs advertising clients in Spain and Peru .

Currently I am counting on flexibility and willingness to continue doing what I love. I invite you to meet me and my designs on this website.
